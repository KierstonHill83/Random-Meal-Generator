About Us


Ever had one of those days when you just don't know what to fix for for breakfast or lunch or dinner? Wouldn't it be wonderful if there was an app that could generate meals automatically...well, there is! With our app, Random Meal Generator, you can click one simple button that will randomly choose a recipe for you.

When you are logged in, click the "Get Recipe" button, enter in the type of dish you would like with an ingredient or multiple ingredients seperated by a comma for a random recipe to be shown. Click the picture to be taken to the site with the directions. If you like the recipe that is shown, click the green approve buttom to save it to your calendar. If you don't like it, click the red cancel button and re-enter in the type and ingredients to get another dish.
